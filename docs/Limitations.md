## Latency

Latency must be as low as possible for smooth operations so that your inputs do what you expect them to do. Keep in mind that we are running this on off-the-shelf commodity hardware over a 4G/LTE network, so we will never compete with dedicated analog or digital FPV systems.

For detailed latency measurements, breakdowns, and optimization tips, see the dedicated [Latency page](Latency.md).

Generally speaking, latency is less of an issue on slower vehicles and more critical on fast-moving vehicles requiring precise control.

## Reception
This plays into latency but needs to be mentioned separately, your mobile reception will have a lot of influence in how the system works. Different providers perform differently in different areas. It is highly recommended to do your research of how well your area is covered by different providers using tools like [cellmapper.net](https://cellmapper.net). See [Antennas](Antennas.md) for a detailed walkthrough on using Cellmapper.
